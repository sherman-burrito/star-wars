Star Wars

The franchise began in 1977 and became a pop culture phenomenom. The sequels to Star Wars were also successful "The Empire Strikes Back" and "Return of the Jedi". The first three films constitute the original Star Wars trilogy. A prequel trilogy was later released between 1999 and 2005, which received a more mixed reaction from critics and fans in comparison to the original trilogy. All seven films were nominated or won Academey Awards.
