Star Wars

Star Wars is an American epic space opera, a film series created by George Lucas. It was released in 1977 and became a pop culture phenomenom. The sequels to Star Wars were also successful "The Empire Strikes Back" and "Return of the Jedi". The first three films constitute the original Star Wars trilogy. A prequel trilogy was later released between 1999 and 2005, which received a more mixed reaction from critics and fans in comparison to the original trilogy. All seven films were nominated or won Academey Awards. Star Wars also holds a Guinness World Records title for the "Most successful film merchandising franchise. In 2012 the value of the Star Wars franchise estimated 30.7 billion.
