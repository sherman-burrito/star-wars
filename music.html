Star Wars Background Music

Music

The primary music in Star Wars is written by composer John Williams. It was performed by the London Symphony Orchestra. John Williams was the composer of all seven Star Wars saga films. Williams returned for the the most recent Star Wars "The Force Awakens". Its was'nt just him this time composing. There was also Gustavo Dudamel.

John Williams

John Towner Williams was on born February 7, 1932. He is an American composer he has composed some of the most popular and recognizable films.
